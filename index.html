About Ollivanders

Ollivanders is a very famous wand shop. Founded in 382 B.C. Located in Diagon Alley. It is owned by the Ollivander family, widely acknowledged to be the best wandmakers in Great Britain. Every wizard paying a visit to the shop will be able to be selected by a great quality wand, whose loyalty won't disappoint.

About our wands

Each wand has a unique core made of a high quality material containing special magic properties. Ollivanders uses three types of core material: unicorn hair, dragon heartstring and phoenix feather. After lots of research, it was discovered that only these three would produce wands of best quality possible. Click here to learn more about wand cores.

Ollivanders also uses many different types of high quality wood to make wands. Each kind of wood gives certain characterists to the final product. Click here to learn more about about wand woods.

The Owner of Ollivanders

Garrick Ollivander is the current proprietor of Ollivanders. He is known as the best wandmaker in Great Britain. He created a completely new method of making wands by choosing high quality wand woods and cores, matching them together, and letting the wand choose its own master. This method proved to be capable of creating powerful wands that serve powerful wizards and witches.
